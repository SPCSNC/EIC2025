Wristband/passes order link

The link below will redirect you to the Facts giving website, where you can order a wristband (pass) for the basketball tournament. 
Please fill out the form and place your order before February 9th.

Wristbands ordered before February 9th will be sent to your child's school. Wristbands ordered after February 9th can be picked  up at the opening ceremony.  Wristbands will also be sold at every game location.

Wristband prices are: $15.00 for the weekend or $5 daily for adults and students 6th grade and up. Students 5th grade and below will be admitted free.
